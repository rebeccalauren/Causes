Blight. Decay. Neglect.

These are not words most Philadelphians would like to use to describe their communities, yet time and time again, abandoned homes and businesses leave otherwise healthy neighborhoods with these negative effects. Declining home values, increased crime, and unsafe structural conditions make abandoned buildings the ultimate shame of our city.

Building for Hope wants to change that.

Beauty. Innovation. Hope.

These are the words we should be using to describe our neighborhoods in Philadelphia. Support Building Hope with your money, your time, and your political action today.
